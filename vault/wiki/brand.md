# Brand — I Got A Dom

**Name:** I Got A Dom  *(never "LLC")*
**Tagline:** Chaos to Clarity.

**Core promise:**
Messy notes, screenshots, menus, and rough content go in. Clean,
professional, ready-to-send results come out. Same day.

**Tone:** Direct. Confident. Human. Fast. Premium.
*NOT corporate. NOT scammy. NOT overpromising.*

**Disclaimer** (always visible in services + footer):
> I Got A Dom does not provide legal, tax, financial, or professional
> advice. I organize facts, clean up writing, and create clear drafts.

## Voice rules
- Speak in the first person ("I"), not "we" or "our team."
- Short sentences. Concrete nouns. No filler, no buzzwords.
- Lead with the outcome (clean, done, same-day), not the process.

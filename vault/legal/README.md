# I Got A Dom — Client Documents & Policies

Plain-language documents I use so every client knows exactly what they get, what I do
with their stuff, and what they own at the end. Two principles run through all of it:
**you own your work** and **I keep your stuff safe and private**.

> ⚠️ **These are plain-language templates, not legal advice.** I Got A Dom is not a law
> firm and does not provide legal, tax, medical, or financial advice. Have a licensed
> attorney in your state (Illinois, for me) review and adapt these before relying on them.

## The short version
- **You own everything** at the end. For a website, ownership of the finished site and its
  source files transfers to you **once you've paid in full.** I keep nothing hostage.
- **You own your domain and hosting accounts** — in your name, with your logins.
- I only take in what I need to do the job, I keep it briefly, then I delete it. I never
  share, sell, or publish your stuff.
- I **can't** accept regulated or high-risk data (patient records, SSNs, full card/bank
  numbers, student records, privileged legal files). Redact it first or don't send it.
- I organize facts and clean up writing. I **don't** give legal, tax, financial, or medical
  advice.

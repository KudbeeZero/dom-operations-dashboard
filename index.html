Resumes, bios, cover letters, meeting notes, screenshots with text in them, menus, flyers, rough email drafts, voice note transcriptions, scattered bullet points — basically anything that's a mess on paper or on a screen. If you're not sure, just ask. I'll tell you straight.

Text me at 773-647-7598 — that's the fastest. You can also fill out the form below. Send whatever you have: a screenshot, a file attachment, a Google Docs link, copied text, a photo. If you can send it in a message, I can work with it.

How long does it take?

Most jobs come back the same day — many within a few hours. If your job is bigger or I'm slammed, I'll tell you upfront. I don't disappear on people.

I fix it. Same day, no argument, no extra charge. Tell me what you want changed and I'll get it right. If you're genuinely unhappy, we'll figure it out — I'm not trying to collect your $25 and leave you with something unusable.

Is my stuff confidential?

Yes. I don't share, sell, publish, or show anyone what you send me. Everything stays between us. I'm not a big company with a data policy — I'm one person who just wants to do good work.

Do you need any logins or passwords?

No. Just send me the document, screenshot, or text. I don't need access to your accounts — ever.

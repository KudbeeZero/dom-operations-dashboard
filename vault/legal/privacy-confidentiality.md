# Privacy & Confidentiality Policy

> ⚠️ **Plain-language template, not legal advice.** I Got A Dom is not a law firm and does
> not provide legal, tax, medical, or financial advice. Have a licensed Illinois attorney
> review and adapt this before you rely on it.

I'm one person, not a big company with a data department. So I keep this simple: I take in
only what I need to do your job, I protect it, I keep it briefly, and then I delete it. I
never share, sell, or publish your stuff.

## What I collect
- **What you send me to work on** — the document, screenshot, notes, or text you want cleaned
  up or built into a site.
- **How to reach you** — your name and a phone number or email, so I can send the work back
  and answer questions.
- That's it. **I don't need and won't ask for logins or passwords to your accounts.**

## What I do with it
- I use your information **only to do the job you hired me for.** Nothing else.
- I **don't sell it, share it, publish it, or show it to anyone.** What you send me stays
  between us.
- I keep it **only as long as I need it** for your job and any quick follow-up fixes, then I
  delete it.

## How I protect it
- Files and messages move over **encrypted connections (TLS)** — the same kind of protection
  your bank's website uses.
- I keep your stuff on a small number of devices/accounts that I control, locked down with
  strong passwords.
- **Please don't email or text me Social Security numbers, full card or bank-account numbers,
  or medical records.** I can't accept those (see the Acceptable Use policy), and email/text
  isn't the place for them anyway. If something sensitive ever truly needs to be handled, we'll
  find a secure way — but for normal document cleanup, you'll never need to send any of that.

## Confidentiality promise
Whatever you send me is confidential. I won't repeat what's in it, won't use it for anything
but your job, and won't add you to any list. If I ever want to show a piece of work as a sample,
I'll ask you first and only use it with your OK (and I'll remove anything that identifies you).

## Honest scope
I organize facts and clean up writing. I **don't** provide legal, tax, financial, or medical
advice.

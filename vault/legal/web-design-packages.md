Simple, honest website packages. Same values as everything else I do: **you own the finished
site, you own your domain and hosting, and there are no surprises.** Same-day isn't promised
for a full site — good sites take a little time — but I'll always give you a real timeline.

> 💡 **Prices marked `$___` are placeholders.** Dominick sets the final numbers. The suggested
> ranges are starting points for a low-cost, solo operator — keep them honest and sustainable.

## 1. One-Pager — *suggested ~$___ (e.g. $150–$300)*
A clean, single-page website. Perfect for a service, a small business, or a personal page.
- One page, mobile-friendly, fast-loading
- Your sections (about, services, contact, etc.)
- A contact method (form, click-to-text, or click-to-call)
- Basic on-page SEO (title, description, so Google can find you)
- **1 round of revisions**
- **You own the finished site and source files on final payment.**

## 2. Multi-Page — *suggested ~$___ (e.g. $400–$800)*
A small multi-page site for a business that needs a few rooms, not just a front door.
- Up to ____ pages (e.g. Home, Services, About, Contact)
- Mobile-friendly, fast-loading
- Contact form + basic SEO on every page
- A simple way for you to make small text edits later (I'll show you how)
- **2 rounds of revisions**
- **You own the finished site and source files on final payment.**

## 3. Care & Updates (optional add-on) — *suggested ~$___/month (e.g. $25–$60)*
Only if you want it. A simple monthly arrangement for small ongoing changes.
- A set amount of small edits per month (text swaps, new photos, price changes)
- I keep an eye on the site so it stays up and current
- **Cancel anytime. No lock-in, no hostage-ware** — it's your site either way.

## How domain & hosting work (every package)
- **You own your domain and hosting**, in your name, with your logins. See
  [Domain & Hosting Ownership](domain-hosting-ownership.md).
- If I buy either one for you, I pass it through **at cost — no markup** — and transfer it into
  your account at the end.
- Typical real-world costs (yours, not mine): a domain runs roughly **$10–$20/year**; simple
  hosting can be **free to ~$10/month** depending on what your site needs. I'll tell you the
  honest options before you spend a dime.

## What every package includes
- A deposit to start (e.g. 50%), balance due at the end before handover
- Honest timeline, told upfront
- **Full ownership handover on final payment** — site, source files, and every login
- No regulated/sensitive data in the project (see the Acceptable Use policy)
- The fix-it promise: if it's not what we agreed on, I make it right

## Honest scope
I build and clean up. I **don't** provide legal, tax, financial, or medical advice, and your
finished site isn't that kind of advice.
